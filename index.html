Gopuram Tamil Calendar – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Gopuram (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is a Tamil calendar application designed to provide Panchangam, Tamil date details, festival information, and other related content. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Gopuram does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information
  • Location data (GPS or background location)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Analytics or usage behavior
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Payment or financial information

2. No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking

The app does not use any analytics tools such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, or any other third-party tracking SDKs.

3. No Ads & No Data Sharing

Gopuram currently does not display ads. Because we do not collect user information, no data is shared with advertisers, ad networks, or external services.

4. Local Data Only

Any settings or preferences inside the app (such as the selected month or display settings) are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers.

5. Children’s Privacy

Gopuram is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is safe for children and complies with Google Play policies.

6. External Links

The app may contain links to external websites for additional information. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app features change in the future. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
