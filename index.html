Gopuram Tamil Calendar – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Gopuram (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is a simple Tamil calendar application designed to provide Panchangam, Tamil date, festival information, and related content. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Gopuram does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Payment or financial information

2. No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking

The app does not use any analytics tools such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, or third-party tracking SDKs.

3. No Ads & No Data Sharing

Gopuram currently does not display ads. No user information is shared with advertisers, ad networks, or external third-party services.

4. Local Data Only

Any preferences or settings (such as selected month or display mode) are stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to our servers.

5. Children’s Privacy

Gopuram is suitable for users of all ages and complies with Google Play policies. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is safe for children.

6. External Links

The app may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app features change. Updated versions will be posted on this page.
